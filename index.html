1. Introduction

As a part of the IsDBW Scholarship Project and Database Design and Development Program, Round-51 (DDD/DTCM-A/51/01) on Module Oracle Forms & Reports we were instructed to do a real-life project. On the grounds of that, we chose the application named “Shopping Mall Management Application System” to work on. At the end of the module, we try to focus on the practical tasks that we applied in the application system. However, this outline will help you to get a light overview of our work and the struggle that we faced during building the application from scratch.

2. About the Application

Shopping Mall Management based on two particular modules.

2.1 Parking Lot Management

This module allows the parking lot for renting purposes for the shopping mall visitors & guests. Management can set hourly rate with applied business logic and collect it by generating a token during entry and invoice on exit.

2.2 Shop Rent Collection

This module allows the management to collect rent of the shops monthly basis as per the agreement with the shops. After collecting the rent they can generate the rent invoice and know the details of dues, payment and total amount.

3. What are the Unique Features of the Application?

Shopping Mall Management Application is combined with some basic and unique features. We applied some unique features for learning purposes and emphasized our effort to make it a real life problem solving application that can make the business easy for its users.

3.2 Report Features

  3. Daily Parking Earning Report

3.3 Advance Reports

  1. Monthly Year Wise Earning From Parking
  2. Monthly Year Wise Earning From Shop Rent

7.4 List of all Functions

1. Ammount_calc

Function for dynamic calculation of parking rate per hour with rate chart.

            CREATE OR REPLACE FUNCTION SHOP.ammount_calc (p_id IN NUMBER)
    RETURN NUMBER
AS
    h         NUMBER;
    m         NUMBER;
    ammount   NUMBER;
    rate      NUMBER;
BEGIN
    SELECT EXTRACT (HOUR FROM (end_time - start_time)),
           EXTRACT (MINUTE FROM (end_time - start_time)),
           RATE_PER_HOUR
      INTO h, m, rate
      FROM parking_details
     WHERE park_dtl_id = p_id;

    IF h = 0 AND m BETWEEN 0 AND 44
    THEN
        ammount := round((rate / 2));
    ELSIF h = 0 AND m BETWEEN 45 AND 59
    THEN
        ammount := round(rate);
    ELSIF h > 0 AND m BETWEEN 45 AND 59
    THEN
        ammount := ((h + 1) * rate);
    ELSIF h > 0 AND m BETWEEN 15 AND 44
    THEN
        ammount := round(((h * rate) + (rate / 2)));
    ELSIF h > 0 AND m BETWEEN 0 AND 14
    THEN
        ammount := round((h * rate));
    ELSE
        NULL;
    END IF;

    RETURN ammount;
END;
